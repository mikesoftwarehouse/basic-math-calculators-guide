## 🚀 Overview

Ratios are a fundamental concept in mathematics used to **compare two or more quantities**. They help us understand relationships between values and are widely applied in everyday situations such as finance, cooking, business, and data analysis.

---

## 📘 What is a Ratio?

A **ratio** shows how one quantity relates to another.

### ➤ Standard Form:

**a : b**

This means “a to b”, where both values are compared directly.

---

## ✏️ Example

**2 : 5**

👉 This means for every **2 units**, there are **5 units** of another quantity.

---

## 🧩 Simplifying Ratios

Ratios can often be simplified just like fractions.

### Example:

**10 : 20**

Divide both numbers by 10 →
**1 : 2 ✅**

---

## 🔄 Ratios and Proportions

A **proportion** shows that two ratios are equal.

Example:

**2 : 5 = 4 : 10**

This is useful in solving real-world problems involving scaling and comparisons.
