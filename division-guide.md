## 🚀 Overview

Division is one of the **four fundamental arithmetic operations** in mathematics. It allows us to split numbers into equal parts and is essential in both academic learning and real-world problem-solving.

From sharing items equally to calculating averages, division plays a critical role in everyday life.

---

## 📘 What is Division?

Division is the process of determining how many times one number (the **divisor**) fits into another number (the **dividend**).

### ➤ Basic Formula:

**Dividend ÷ Divisor = Quotient**

---

## ✏️ Example

Let’s solve a practical example:

**11275 ÷ 25 = 451**

👉 This means 25 fits into 11275 exactly **451 times**.

---

## 🧩 Step-by-Step Explanation

Here’s how division works conceptually:

1. Start from the leftmost digits of the dividend
2. Determine how many times the divisor fits
3. Multiply and subtract
4. Bring down the next digit
5. Repeat until all digits are used

This method is especially useful in **long division problems**.
